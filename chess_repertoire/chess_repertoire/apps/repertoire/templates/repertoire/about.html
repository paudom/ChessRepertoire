About Chess Repertoire

Chess Repertoire is a tool designed for studying and storing the repertoire of a player, saving variations of each opening. It also includes some features for reviewing, practicing and creating new variations.

Openings

In the case of openings, we have classified them by difficulty and category. Difficulty meaning how difficult is to play the opening ranging from Beginner to Advanced.

Variations

In the case of variations, we have classified them by Category. Theoric is granted to variations with deep theory analysis. Positional is given when the variation leads to very positional games. Tricky is assigned to variations with very tricky positions. Sharp variations are those where there are no many good moves. Advantageous means that the other player is already in a bad shape.
